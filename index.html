Let's Get Started!

Step 1

Register Your AirSonea™ Device

After registering your device, you will receive an email to confirm a registration followed by an email with a link to download your AirSonea and AsthmaSense apps for free.

Why Two Apps? Register Your Device

Step 2

Download

Make sure you've downloaded the required apps, AsthmaSense and AirSonea. If you haven't downloaded the apps, the links below will take you to the download of your choice.

AirSonea

AsthmaSense

Step 3

Power On

To take your first measurement, power on the AirSonea device by sliding the Power Slide Button.

  • To pair the AirSonea device, you will need to navigate to the Bluetooth® settings on your smartphone.
  • This should activate the AirSonea app on your phone. If not, see TROUBLESHOOTING.
  • The AirSonea device should now be "paired".

Step 4

Your First Measurement

To start the measurement, place the sensor over the windpipe near the base of the throat, as shown on the HINT screen.

(Tap HINT in your app for directions on where to place the device.)

Tap the AirSonea logo to start your measurement.

Step 5

Count Down

The AirSonea app will count down to the end of your screening.

Your WheezeRATE™ data will be transmitted to the cloud.

After analysis in the Cloud, your WheezeRATE results will appear on the smartphone screen. WheezeRATE is shown as a % of recorded breathing time that you are wheezing.

Step 6

AsthmaSense® Cloud

AirSonea will prompt AsthmaSense Cloud to open and store your WheezeRATE.

The AsthmaSense system makes it easy to share asthma information with your family or physician.

Never delay treatment if you are having symptoms of asthma.

Your Personal Asthma Security System is now fully set up.

By monitoring your wheeze, you can now start managing your asthma worries.
